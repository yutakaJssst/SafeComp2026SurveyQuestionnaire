# Survey Questionnaire: Adoption of Safety Standards in the Japanese Automotive Industry

This document describes the questionnaire used in the study "Adoption of Safety Standards in the Japanese Automotive Industry" (Matsuno, Ochiai, Kono, SafeComp 2026). The survey was distributed to the JASPAR functional safety working group in December 2025.

## Response Scales

**Likert scale (5-point agreement)**:
1. Strongly disagree / 全くそう思わない
2. Disagree / あまりそう思わない
3. Neither agree nor disagree / どちらとも言えない
4. Agree / そう思う
5. Strongly agree / 非常にそう思う
- N/A: Not applicable or cannot evaluate / 該当なし・評価できない

**V-model role allocation scale (5-point OEM--Supplier)**:
1. Entirely OEM / 完全にOEM
2. Mainly OEM / 主にOEM
3. Equal / 半々
4. Mainly Supplier / 主にサプライヤー
5. Entirely Supplier / 完全にサプライヤー
- N/A: Not applicable / 該当なし

**Boundary clarity scale (5-point)**:
1. Much less clear than ISO 26262 / 非常に曖昧
2. Somewhat less clear / やや曖昧
3. Same as ISO 26262 / 同程度
4. Somewhat clearer / やや明確
5. Much clearer than ISO 26262 / 非常に明確
- Don't know / わからない

## Notes

- The survey was administered as a web-based questionnaire via Microsoft Forms.
- Branching logic was applied: Sections 2--4 detailed items (adoption drivers, benefit/cost, V-model roles) were shown only to respondents who selected "Active use" or "Preparing" for the corresponding standard.
- All responses were anonymous; respondents were instructed to answer based on individual practical experience rather than official company positions.
- The original questionnaire was in Japanese. English translations in this document are by the authors.
